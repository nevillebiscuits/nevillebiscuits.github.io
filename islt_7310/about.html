Education

I attended East Noble High School in Indiana. There I achieved a 4.0, in addition to being the Editor in Chief of the yearbook. After high school, I went to Indiana Wesleyan University where I received a Bachelor of Science degree in English Education and graduated Magna Cum Laude.


--

Professional Career

After college, I returned to East Noble, this time as a teacher. Here I gained experience in freshman, sophomore, and junior English, as well as AP Language and Creative Writing. In 2015 I moved to St. Louis and subbed for a year before being hired as an 8th grade ELA teacher at Parkway West Middle School. I teach there presently with an amazing group of educators.

Continuing Education

Last spring, I began taking classes from The University of Missouri, seeking a Master of Science degree in Learning Technologies and Design with a Technology in Schools emphasis. I anticipate graduating in late 2021.
